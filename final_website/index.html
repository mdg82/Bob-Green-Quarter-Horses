About Us

We are trying to produce the best cow horses that we can, with emphasis on cutting and roping. We have tried to build a brood mare herd of daughters and granddaughters of top studs. Currently, we have own daughters of “Rooster”, and High Brow Cat in the herd. We have granddaughters of Doc O’lena, Peppy San Badger, Big Red Solano, Mr Gun Smoke, Smart Little Lena, High Brow Hickory, Peptoboonsmal, Mecom Blue, Hollywood Dun It, Smart Chic Olena, and Topsail Whiz in the herd. Our main stud is Pequito Pep, an own son of High Brow CD and a Dual Pep daughter.

Come by and see us, sometime!
